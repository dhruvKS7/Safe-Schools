Welcome to Safe Schools!

Safe Schools is a platform dedicated to helping you find the best, safest schools in LA. We understand that your child's safety is of the utmost importance, and our sole aim is to help you make the most informed decision possible. We have an extensive list of schools in LA, and each one has key information. This includes a user rating - an average on a scale of 1-10 representing a school's academic quality - and a safety rating - a numerical value between 0 and 100 representing a school's overall safety.
